Frequently Asked Questions

In a nutshell, what is this thing?

Put simply, Everpresent is a tool that posts content to Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn on your behalf at times you choose. For example, I input a few blog posts, a links to a sales page, a motivational quote or image into Everpresent's "Queue" page. The content will then be tweeted, facebooked or posted to LinkedIn (or posted to a combinmation of all three) according to the pre-determined schedule that I have previously set.

An how is this going to help me?

Instead of spamming your followers with 10 posts at once, or worse, hiring someone to operate your social media for you, Everpresent will spread the content over your working week leaving you free to carry on hustling for your business. It's a cost effective way to sustain regular updates of your social media.

How does Evepresent work?

Once you sign up to Everpresent you can choose which social media accounts you wish to control. The application then establishes a connection via an API to each social channel you choose. It then moves through the pre-set schedule and once the time comes for that post to go out, it contacts each social media channel you've selected it to go to and requests the information to be posted.

And what about my privacy, I don't want you having free reign on my social accounts.

At no stage do we see your any of your social media passwords. We never actually log into to any accounts. The connection is made similar to that of a firewall, we create an exception and the communication is that of the post only. We never have access to check your Facebook messages to see what you're up to this weekend. But feel free to let us know, maybe we can catch up.

So if you're controlling my posts, what's stopping you from posting whatever you want?

We will never post anything other than what you tell us. And when we say "what you tell us" we mean the app itself You remain in complete control of your posts. On the flipside, we can't be held responsible for spelling mistakes, or posts that may offend others because your the only one who controls the posts. But play nice.
